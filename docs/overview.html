Resources

The Ocean Health Index has two main websites – oceanhealthindex.org and ohi-science.org – each with their own purpose. As a general rule of thumb, oceanhealthindex.org gives an overview of OHI, while ohi-science.org provides a technical perspective of OHI and insights into the open data science that supports the creation of OHI scores.

  • http://ohi-science.org/
  • http://www.oceanhealthindex.org
